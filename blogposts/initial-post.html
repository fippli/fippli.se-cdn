After thinking and thinking about what to write in my initial post I figured out that it's best to jut rant about something. For example that Apple and Google uses the COVID-19 crisis as an opportunity to gather more data from smartphone users around the world.

As described here https://www.apple.com/newsroom/2020/04/apple-and-google-partner-on-covid-19-contact-tracing-technology/ they have a goal to build this functionalty into their operating systems. I guess it's the right time for them to introduce these ideas now when people are still worried and in fear of what will happen with this pandemic.
